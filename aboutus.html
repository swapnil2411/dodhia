Dodhia Group

Four decades of delivering innovation,
quality & new possibilities

For over four decades, Dodhia Group has established itself as a leading manufacturer of yarns and chemicals in India and a prominent plastic recycling company. As a family-owned business, we pride ourselves on our values, corporate culture, and commitment to setting industry benchmarks. Led by an experienced Board of Directors and a highly qualified network of advisors and consultants, our vision is to become the definitive market leader in all our business segments  in India and abroad.
We are also excited to expand into a wide range of products made from recycled plastic, paving the way for a truly circular economy.
