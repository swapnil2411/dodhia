What is Petopoly ?

Trashed PET bottles that could have been choking marine life or filling up landfills are collected inspected,stripped of their caps and labels, and granulated into small plastic flakes. Then through a proprietary mechanical process of polymerization, the granules are dried and made into a ploymer fiber called PetopolyTM.
