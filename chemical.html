About

Dodhia
Chem-Tex

At Dodhia Chem-Tex Pvt. Ltd., our specialty and pharmaceutical research centre, we firmly believe that change is the only constant and so we innovate perpetually.

We, M/s. Dodhia Chem-Tex Pvt. Ltd. hereby takes the opportunity to introduce ourselves as one of the leading manufacturers and exporters of Calcium Hypochlorite 65% and 70% in India. “Hi-Clear” is our registered brand for high-quality Calcium Hypochlorite. Our Calcium Hypochlorite is made through the latest sodium process technology for higher purity and stability. Dodhia Chem-Tex Pvt. Ltd. is also into manufacturing TCCA and Calcium Formate in India.

Kemox Corporation is one of the leading manufacturers of Sodium Permanganate 40% solution, textile specialty chemicals, leather chemicals, Oil field chemicals, paper industry chemicals, and other performance-based products.

Our R&D team - in close collaboration with the clients- develop quality, high performance and cost-effective chemical products for their markets.
