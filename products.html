Dyed textured yarn (DTY)

DSL textured yarn offers uniform crimp & softness, it is available in Crimped, stretch, NIM, IM, LIM, SIM, HIM, etc. This makes it most suitable for seams for all industrial applications. It provides a good seam cover & unmatched softness. It performs best in wash fastness, rub fastness, hypochlorite fastness and dry cleaning fastness.

  • PLY/Cabled :

    2 to 20 ply & cabled for deniers up to 10000 or more

  • Deniers :

    300 to 20000

  • Luster :

    Semi-dull , Full dull , Bright , cationic

  • Microfilaments :

    144 , 288 , 576 filaments or more

  • Quality :

    Non-Intermingled , Semi-Intermingled , Intermingled & Twisted

  • PLY/Cabled :

    2 to 20 ply & cabled for deniers up to 10000 or more

These yarns can be produced in raw white, package-dyed, dope-dyed, space-dyed & Recycled PetopolyTM

Air textured yarn (ATY)

ATY can offer an entirely different look and hand to any fabric. It can be used in a very broad range of fabrics from simple lightweight scrim all the way to very heavy-duty soft luggage fabrics, from lightweight swimwear to heavy-weight parkas. Several filament yarns can be mixed to construct the ideal ATY for certain endues.

  • Deniers :

    70 to 2000

  • Microfilaments :

    144 , 288 , 576 filaments or more

  • Luster :

    Semi-dull , Bright , Cationic & Full dyed

  • Quality :

    Non-Intermingled , Semi-Intermingled , Intermingled & Twisted

  • PLY/Cabled :

    2 to 20 ply & cabled for deniers up to 10000 or more

These yarns can be produced in Raw White , Cone dyed , Dope dyed & , Space dyed.
