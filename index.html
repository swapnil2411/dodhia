WE ARE DODHIA GROUP

The Dodhia Group has been at the forefront of innovation and expansion, achieving meteoric growth over the past decade. The company is among the most prominent players in the textiles, chemicals, and plastic recycling industries in India with a global footprint.

With a focus on utilizing the latest technology for innovation and quality, Dodhia is committed to creating new possibilities that deliver delightful customer experiences and lead the way towards a more sustainable future.
